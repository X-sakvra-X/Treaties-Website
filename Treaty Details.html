Welcome to Tiny Treaty Tales

Here you will find a collection of fun stories and information about the treaties that were signed between the Indigenous peoples of Canada and the British Crown. These treaties have had a lasting impact on the relationship between Indigenous and non-Indigenous peoples in Canada, and it is important to understand their history and significance.

Explore the site to learn more about the treaties, the people involved, and the impact they have had on the land and its people. We hope you enjoy your visit!

Why Is Treaty 1 Still Important?

Treaty 1 is still a promise we remember today.

  • 🤝 We try to keep the promise and be fair.
  • 🎓 Kids learn about Treaty 1 in school.
  • 🏘️ First Nations people still live on Treaty 1 land.
  • 💬 People talk and learn how to be better friends.

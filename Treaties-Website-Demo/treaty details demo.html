What Did Treaty 1 Promise?

In 1871, First Nations leaders and the Queen’s helpers made a big promise.

  • 👣 First Nations would share the land.
  • 🛖 They could keep living on their land in special areas called reserves.
  • 🎒 The government would build schools for children.
  • 🛠️ Help would be given for farming and tools.

Welcome to Our Treaty 1 Learning Space!

Treaty 1 is an important agreement that shapes the land we live on today. This website is a place for young learners in grades 1-3 to explore, understand, and appreciate the history and meaning of Treaty 1 in a fun and engaging way! Through stories, activities, and pictures, we’ll learn about the Indigenous peoples who signed the treaty, the promises that were made, and why treaties still matter today. Come along on this journey as we discover the past, understand the present, and respect the future—together!
